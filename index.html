Projects

BTSNE M.A.T. Time

Beat the Streets New England is a sports-based youth development nonprofit that is quickly growing in reach throughout the region, and needed an effective way to teach the core skills and values needed in the organization to all of the new employees, volunteers, and board members that join BTSNE as it grows. To satisfy this need, I designed the visuals, animation, content, and experience for M.A.T. Time, a short onboarding course that provides the materials needed to succeed in the organization.

NewCo Oncology Database

Founded by a pair of PhD Graduates from MIT and a design consultant and teacher at Williams College, NewCo is a startup that offered a solution for oncologists to have access to a more complete database of cancer cases that could easily be reviewed, discussed and updated. My task was to solve the problem of creating an interface that would be easy and intuitive, without imposing unnecessary structure on busy professional oncologists.

ThinkCERCA

ThinkCERCA is an industry leader in the K-12 EdTech space. Based out of Chicago, ThinkCERCA provides a tool used for teaching reading, writing, and literacy in several school districts around the country. The platform was struggling to see user engagement with the student-side and admin dashboards, so I undertook the task of redesigning the visuals and experience of each. I began with market research of dashboards within the EdTech industry, and used my findings to inform the structure of my design. My designs are currently set to be tested with customers to gain further insights on the experience of the platform.

About Me

Hello! Thanks for stopping by. Welcome to my Portfolio. My name is Dante. I'm currently studying philosophy and cognitive science at Williams College, along with UI, UX and Interaction Design. I've had a somewhat nontraditional journey into the field of design over the past few years. I have always been fascinated by human decision making, the natural world, and the ways that the smallest of details can influence a person's thoughts and actions. On top of that, I grew up as a kid of the digital world. Figuring out to solve everyday problems around my house with software, connecting with my friends online, and constantly learning about the world led me to spend a lot of time with digital interfaces, like many in my generation. Now, I am spending my off time trying to learn every industry skill I can, as I explore potential opportunities going forward. My experience as a college athlete has taught me commitment and focus that I am eager to channel into my career as I develop day after day. I am looking to take on adventurous new projects, learn from excellent mentors, and create the best interactive products I can.
