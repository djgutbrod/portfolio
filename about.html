About Dante Gutbrod

Dante Gutbrod is a highly skilled UX and Product Designer with a strong background in creating exceptional user experiences for various industries, including edTech, healthcare, and eCommerce. A natural leader and problem solver, Dante boasts a diverse skill set that spans digital creation and communication.

With a Bachelor of Arts from Williams College, Dante's academic career includes coursework in design thinking, user experience, psychology, computer programming, cognitive science, and visual perception. Dante further expanded his expertise through Interaction Design Foundation courses, focusing on UX and interaction design.

Dante has an impressive work history, having served as a Freelance UX Design Consultant, UX Research/Product Design Intern at ThinkCERCA, and Lead Program Advisor and Associate Board Member for Beat the Streets New England. Dante has also worked as an Education Intern at Mystic Aquarium, where he developed curriculum and designed educational materials.

In addition to his work experience, Dante has successfully completed several design projects and holds certifications from Interaction Design Foundation and Google. Dante is highly proficient in a wide range of tools and technologies, such as Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop, Sketch, User Personas, Information Architecture, Graphic Design, User-centered Design, Mockups, Wireframing, Prototyping, CSS, HTML, Python, and R.
